Rhythm Ship

In this project, my partner and I made a rhythm game settled in outer space. Using the arrow keys, play to the beat of the music to evade meteors and shoot down enemy spaceships.

The Artifact

As a team of 6, we created a first person shooter, platforming game. The first level is where the user must jump over the crates to reach the ladder to move on to the second level. In the second level, enemy pirates board the ship and the user must eliminate them and reach the escape pod.

Game Scenery

We created a scene in 3D that the user can walk around. It is an island made of blocks, similar to minecraft, with randomized terrain every time the user runs the scene.
